The "Sniff Test": Visual & Stylistic Clues
AI models are statistical predictors, often resulting in writing that is "average" and overly polished.

The "AI Vocabulary": Look for the overuse of specific words that AI models favor heavily:

Verbs: "Delve," "leverage," "navigate," "unlock," "foster," "showcase."

Nouns: "Landscape," "tapestry," "realm," "testament," "nuance," "ecosystem."

Adjectives: "Seamless," "vibrant," "pivotal," "transformative," "crucial."
